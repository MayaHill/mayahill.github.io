About Me

Hi, I'm Maya, a 19-year-old aspiring gameplay programmer from Montpellier, France! Currently studying as a 3rd year student at EPITECH and pursuing a master's degree in Computer Science, I've chosen to specialise myself in game programming.

In those 3 years, I've been able to experience many events such as hackathons, game jams, a Game Camp, as well as a 5 month internship at TheGameBakers!
I've also had the opportunity to participate in and then run the school's GDC (Game Dev Club) and become an Educational Assistant to share my skills and experience with younger students.

My main programming languages are C, C++ and C# and I'm most comfortable with Unity and Godot, but I'm always up for a new challenge.

I hope to continue to improve myself, learn new skills and meet new people along the way!
